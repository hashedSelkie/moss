Our aim

Our aim The Friends of Lenzie Moss is a voluntary association dedicated to conserving the Moss for the benefit of present and future generations by all possible means: through education, liaison with official bodies, and appropriate environmental management. Details of recent activities and work on Lenzie Moss can be found in our NEWS page.
